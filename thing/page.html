        ADVICE FOR BOOKING

        • If you are booking for 15+ people this form is not for you. Please contact moveitgroups@speeddata.co.uk to access the group booking form and benefit from your group discount

        • The MOVE IT box office is a live booking form which means that there may be other people trying to buy dance classes at the same time as you. The classes in your shopping basket are not confirmed until you have paid for them
        • A £2 booking fee will apply per transaction in advance (not per ticket)
